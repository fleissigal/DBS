#### When designing a bedroom I pick:
----

1. Flooring
	1. Flooring Type
	2. Flooring Style
2. Walls
	1. Wall Material
	2. Wall Covering
	3. Wall Covering Style
3. Ceiling - same as walls, maybe different colours
4. Moulding
	1. Moulding Type
	2. Moulding Finish
5. Lighting
	1. Number of lights
	2. Lighting Locations
	3. Lighting Styles
6. Plugs & Switches (maybe not selectable)
